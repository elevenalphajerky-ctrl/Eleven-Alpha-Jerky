Our Story

Built Through Family, Tradition, and Years of Refinement.

More Than a Snack

Eleven Alpha Jerky wasn’t built overnight. What started over 15 years ago as a passion for perfecting jerky has turned into something much bigger—rooted in bold flavor, consistent quality, and a handcrafted approach.

Over the years, recipes were tested, adjusted, and refined through countless batches—balancing smoke, seasoning, heat, texture, and tenderness until each flavor delivered on its name.

At the center of Eleven Alpha Jerky is family. The goal was never just to make jerky—it was to build something meaningful that reflected discipline, consistency, hard work, and pride in the finished product.

Every batch is our commitment, and our job is to stand behind the product we put our name on. Our family’s feedback helped shape these flavors into what they are today.

Eleven Alpha Jerky represents more than a snack. It represents years of refinement, family support, long hours, and the mindset of building something the right way—one batch at a time.
